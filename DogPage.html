My Dog

My dog Cooper is my best friend. He likes to chase the soccer ball when I practice. It is so funny to see him running and trying to fit the ball in his mouth, as it does not fit.

Once Cooper saved my life. When I was only 4 years old. I started to go into a street to get a ball that had rolled across the street. Of course I did not think to look for cars! Cooper got in front of me and knocked me backwards onto the sidewalk. I was mad until I saw the car go by where I would have been. Then I was happy that Cooper was smarter than I was.
